Global temperature record shows 2016 as the warmest year on record

The article layout now keeps line length controlled, image captions readable, and tabular data structured with proper headings.

The global temperature record shows the fluctuations of the temperature of the atmosphere and the oceans through various spans of time. The recent data published by NASA is among the most detailed information available since 1850, when methodical thermometer-based records began.

Sixteen of the 17 warmest years in the 136-year record occurred since 2001, with the exception of 1998. The year 2016 ranks as the warmest on record.
